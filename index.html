  Our Story

Fueled by
Fire & Flavor

Candela means "fire" in Papiamento — and that's exactly what you get here. For over two decades, Candela Food Trailer has been the heartbeat of Oranjestad's late-night food scene, parked right off L.G. Smith Boulevard across from Royal Plaza Mall.

From locals grabbing a bite after a long shift to tourists discovering the island's most legendary steak & cheese platter, Candela serves everyone with the same fiery passion. Incredible sauces, generous portions, and prices that make sense — every single night.

You can also skip the line and order via WhatsApp. Grab Aruban classics like sopito, steak & cheese, crazy fries, and the iconic conch sandwich — all made fresh, every night.

  What We Serve

The Menu

Something for every craving — from Aruban street classics to late-night guilty pleasures. Ask about daily specials when you visit or order.

Steak & Cheese

The legendary house classic. Juicy steak, melted cheese, and that unforgettable Candela sauce — people have been coming back for this for 20 years.

Fan Favorite

Conch Sandwich

Fresh conch in a perfectly toasted bun. A true taste of the Caribbean that keeps seafood lovers coming back night after night.

Must Try

Crazy Fries

Loaded fries smothered in Candela's signature sauces. Mix and match the garlic sauce, Candela sauce, and pink sauce for the ultimate experience.

Late Night Go-To

Wings

Honey BBQ mini basket wings fried to perfection. Crispy outside, juicy inside — the perfect late-night companion.

Burgers & Hot Dogs

Handcrafted burgers and loaded dogs — including the iconic grilled cheese hot dog. Stacked with flavor and our house-made toppings.

Aruban Classics

Traditional Aruban soup (sopito) and local dishes made with love. Real island flavors that go beyond the beach.
